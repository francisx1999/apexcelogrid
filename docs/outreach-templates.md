## Tips

- **Personalize the first line** to the specific org — reference something they actually do.
- **Lead with the demo link**, not the code — let them *see* it before they read anything.
- **One clear ask** per message (a call, a share, a grant fit) — not several.
- **Follow up once** after ~a week if there's no reply; then move on.
- Keep a simple list of who you contacted and when.
